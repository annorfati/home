01, soi-même

heart child
that's what i call you.

when i look into your eyes
the aroma of wonder
fills the space between us

buried beneath the surface
are bright marbles filled with constellations,
traces of a secret world

gently rippling over your skin,
tracing the lines of your palm
i wonder —

where do you end
and where do i begin?
